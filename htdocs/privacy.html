Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

This privacy policy describes how MNK Solutions LLC ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information through the Supertrainer mobile application ("the App").

What we collect

The App may collect the following information as entered by coaching staff:

  • Player names
  • Wellness check-in responses
  • Injury reports
  • Drill and practice session data

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or any information beyond what is directly entered into the App by its users.

How we use your data

Data entered into the App is used solely to provide coaching and team management functionality. When connected to the internet, data is synced to our cloud backend (Formation), operated by MNK Solutions LLC, to enable access across devices and provide backup.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The App contains no advertisements, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking tools.

Data storage

Synced data is stored on servers located in the United States. Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

Note on website hosting: This marketing website (mnksolutions.dev) is hosted by Gandi on servers located in France. This applies only to the static pages you are currently viewing. No Supertrainer app data, account data, or personal information from users is stored on or transmitted through this website — all app data continues to be stored in the United States as described above.

Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for use by coaching staff, not minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@mnksolutions.dev.
